Our Mission

Adding life to days, not days to life

"Adding life to days"

To improve the quality of life of people suffering from prolonged debilitating illness by providing the best possible holistic care made affordable and acceptable through community participation and also helping the affected families.

Compassionate Care

Providing emotional and spiritual support alongside medical care

Community Driven

Built by the community, for the community

Free Service

Our Impact

Making a difference in the community

We care where you are, The journey starts here

Our home care teams visit patients in their homes with medicines and all necessary medical equipment. We provide wheelchairs, crutches, and other aids free of cost to those in need.

📷 Home care team visiting a patient's family

Wherever the art of medicine is loved, there is also a love of humanity

We perform procedures like injections and catheterization during home visits, saving patients from hospital visits. We guide families in wound care, medication, and diet.

📷 Medical professionals providing compassionate care

Palliative care includes physical, spiritual, emotional elements

We understand the mental, emotional, and social problems faced by patients, their family members, and caregivers, providing comprehensive support.

📷 Holistic care approach - mind, body, and spirit

Stories from the heart - Volunteers are integral to our care

For financially backward families, we provide monthly rice kits and educational assistance ranging from Rs 300 to Rs 2000 for their children.

📷 Community volunteers supporting families in need
